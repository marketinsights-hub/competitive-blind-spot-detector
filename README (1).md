## Example Output (Condensed)

**Idea being tested**
"We're entering the mid-market segment with our AI project management tool. Our main competitors are Asana and Monday. We win on AI automation. We're raising Series B in 6 months."

### Blind Spot Scorecard

| Lens | Risk | The Question You Need to Answer |
|------|------|----------------------------------|
| Invisible Competitor | 🟡 Medium | What are mid-market teams using *right now* instead of you — and how sticky is it? |
| Narrative Trap | 🔴 High | If your name was removed from your homepage, would a buyer know in 10 seconds why you — not Asana — solve their problem? |
| Timing Assumption | 🔴 High | What has changed in your category in the last 6 months that you haven't fully incorporated into your roadmap? |
| Customer Concentration | 🟡 Medium | What do your churned accounts have in common? Have you talked to them in the last 90 days? |
| Moat Mirage | 🟡 Medium | If Monday hired 5 engineers tomorrow to copy your core AI feature — how long until they ship something good enough? |

### Top Risk
**Narrative Trap** — "AI automation" is a feature claim, not a positioning statement. Every competitor in this category is saying the same thing. Buyers can't distinguish you from the noise.

**What to do this week:** Pull your last 5 lost deals. Ask: how did the buyer describe your product to their team? If it doesn't match your positioning, you have a narrative problem before you have a sales problem.

## What This Skill Does

The skill runs a structured analysis in two steps:

**Intake** — you describe your market, your top competitors, and how you currently win deals.

**5 Blind Spot Lenses** — Claude analyzes your input through the angles founders most consistently miss:

1. **Invisible Competitor** — the workaround, the spreadsheet, the internal tool your customer built last quarter
2. **Narrative Trap** — your positioning as you see it vs. how buyers actually hear it
3. **Timing Assumption** — what's changed in your market that you haven't updated your strategy for
4. **Customer Concentration Illusion** — your best customers are not your market; your churned customers contain the real signal
5. **Moat Mirage** — your advantage as a defensible moat vs. a feature a competitor can copy in 90 days

**Output includes:**
- Blind Spot Scorecard (5 lenses, risk-rated)
- Top 3 risks ranked by urgency with one concrete action each
- One uncomfortable question the founder is probably not asking

## Who This Is For

SaaS and AI founders at Series A or B who are:

- Preparing for a funding round and need to stress-test their market narrative
- Seeing a new well-funded competitor and wondering what they're missing
- Entering a new segment or geography and want to pressure-test assumptions
- Getting pushback from investors and can't pinpoint where the story breaks

This is not a data report. It is a structured stress test of what you already know — surfacing the assumptions you're implicitly relying on but haven't examined.
